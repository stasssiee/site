Course Description

AP Physics C Electricity and Magnetism course is equivalent to two one-semester calculus-based college-level physics courses. These courses are especially appropriate for students planning to specialize or major in physical science or engineering. Electricity and magnetism explores topics such as electrostatics; conductors, capacitors, and dielectrics; electric circuits; magnetic fields; and electromagnetism.
